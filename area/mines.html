La Descubridora
    (The Discovery)

The historical references are inconsistent about the sequence of events, but all agree that La Descubridora was the first silver discovery in the Sierra Catorce. 
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
El Negrito

The El Negrito Mine is located high above and to the southeast of the "Pueblo Fantasma" area.  It was notible for having a picturesque chapel. ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
El Milagro

The El Milagro Mine is on the old road southeast and above the San Augustin mine. ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
